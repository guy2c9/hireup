Description
Overview
This Change Request outlines the design and implementation of a custom Pay Advice solution developed by 2cloudnine to meet Hireup’s specific payroll reporting requirements. The solution enhances the standard Pay Advice output by providing a clearer, consolidated, and more informative breakdown of pay details, including shift-level information and consolidated pay rates, while improving the overall presentation and usability of the document. This requirement includes more than display of discretionary allowances and as is significantly more complex than was initially identified from Discovery.
The custom design ensures that Hireup employees receive Pay Advices that are easier to interpret, accurately reflect how pay has been calculated, and align with Hireup’s existing payroll and award structures.
High Level Actions
	•	Design and development of a custom Pay Advice layout tailored to Hireup’s requirements.
	•	Implementation of new payroll logic to support consolidated pay rate presentation.
	•	Extension of Pay Advice data sourcing to include shift-level information.
	•	Redesign of the Pay Advice format to improve clarity, readability, and visual consistency.
	•	Testing and validation to ensure accuracy of calculations, data presentation, and alignment with Hireup’s payroll rules.
Product Changes Required
To support the custom Pay Advice requirements, the solution introduces three key product and design enhancements:
1. Consolidation of Pay Codes to Present a Single Pay Rate
New payroll logic has been introduced to consolidate multiple underlying pay components—specifically standard base rates, discretionary allowance, first aid allowance and associated casual loading—into a single consolidated payment line on the Pay Advice.
	•	Casual loading is programmatically combined with the base rate for display purposes, ensuring employees see one clear, all-inclusive pay rate rather than multiple fragmented lines.
	•	This consolidation is applied at the Pay Advice level only and does not alter the underlying payroll calculations or accounting treatment.
	•	The approach satisfies Hireup’s requirement for simplified pay rate visibility while preserving accuracy and traceability within the payroll engine.


2. Inclusion of Shift-Level Details in Pay Advice Summaries
The Pay Advice has been enhanced to draw directly from shift and timesheet data to provide greater transparency into how pay has been calculated.
	•	Shift-level information, including date and shift times, is displayed within the Pay Advice summary sections.
	•	Units worked, applicable rates, and total amounts are aligned to the relevant shifts and pay codes.
	•	This design allows employees to easily reconcile their Pay Advice against their worked shifts, reducing ambiguity and payroll-related enquiries.
3. Redesigned Pay Advice Layout and Presentation
The Pay Advice document has been redesigned to deliver a cleaner, more modern, and more readable format than Hireup’s legacy solution.
	•	Information hierarchy has been improved to clearly separate earnings summaries, shift breakdowns, and totals.
	•	Redundant or unclear fields have been removed or restructured to reduce visual clutter.
	•	The overall layout has been designed to improve comprehension for employees while maintaining consistency with Hireup’s branding and communication standards.
